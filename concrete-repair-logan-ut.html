Fix It Right, Not Just for Now

Cache Valley is hard on concrete. Freeze-thaw cycles, road salt, washed-out soil and poor original base prep all lead to cracking, spalling and sunken slabs. The mistake we see most often is patching the surface without addressing what's underneath — which means the problem comes right back. We diagnose the cause first, then recommend the most cost-effective fix, whether that's leveling, resurfacing or a full replacement.

We repair and level concrete throughout Logan, North Logan, Smithfield, Providence, Hyrum, Nibley, Hyde Park, Wellsville and the rest of Cache County.

Concrete Repair FAQ

Can you raise sunken concrete instead of replacing it?

Often, yes. If the slab is still structurally sound, leveling (mudjacking or foam lifting) raises it back into place for far less than a tear-out. We tell you honestly which option makes sense for your slab.

Why does concrete crack and sink here?

Freeze-thaw, poor base prep, soil washing out under the slab, and tree roots are the usual culprits in Cache Valley. We fix the cause, not just the surface.

What does repair cost?

It depends on the area and whether it needs leveling, resurfacing or full replacement — leveling is usually the most affordable. We give a free written estimate after seeing it.
